Preschool education

preschool education system is well developed in Belarus. Although it is not compulsory, most children attend kindergarten before going to school. The coverage of children from 1 to 6 years of age with preschool educational institutions is 74.4%, including in rural areas - 49.7%, in urban areas - 81.4%. In the 2015/2016 academic year, 3,972 preschool institutions function in the Republic of Belarus, of which 11 are private, the contingent of pupils is 410.5 peopthosanudle.

General secondary education

teach secondary education and vocational training. Those interested can get a general secondary education by continuing their studies at school. The certificate of general secondary or secondary specialized education is the main document that gives the right to enter a higher educational institution. In the 2015/2016 academic year, 3,233 institutions of general secondary education operate in the Republic of Belarus, of which 10 are private ownership, the student population is 964.9 thousand people. Among the latest trends in the general secondary education system is the introduction of specialized education in high school.

Higher education

Higher education, includes 52 institutions of higher education (universities, academies, institutes), including 9 private ownership. annual admission is over 60 thousand people, the graduation is over 80 thousand specialists.Training of specialists is carried out in 15 profiles of education, including 382 specialties of higher education of the first stage and 331 specialties of higher education of the second stage. Higher education in the Republic of Belarus can be obtained in full-time (full-time, evening) or part-time, including distance, forms.
